What is HealthDataInsights?

HealthDataInsights is a tool to make it easy to understand a NHS Data. The initial intention is to support GP practices to make informed decisions on their recruitment strategies by being able to benchmark themselves with others.

Where does the data come from?

Data on the makeup of the GP workforce is collected from every GP practice in England. NHS Digital provides this data publicly. I have taken the releases from 2015-2021 and joined them into a single (massive) file that powers this website. 

How does this compare to the NHS Digital Dashboard?

NHS Digital provides a General Practice Workforce interactive dashboard. While a useful tool, it doesn’t allow easy benchmarking or viewing of historical trends, which are the main aims of this tool. They are both based on the same underlying data.

Can I ask something or make a suggestion?

Sure! The tool is open source. You can fork it and do your own thing, or drop a suggestion in the issues log. You can also reach out on twitter.
